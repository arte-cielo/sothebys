Overview

Sotheby’s Collections sales are renowned for bringing together eclectic groups of furniture, decorative and fine art from private collections and our autumn instalment is no exception. Highlights include a group from Italy, which offers a colourful array of Chinese objects alongside Italian classics; a charming collection of early oak and English Country furniture adds a folk flavour; whilst a rich assortment of tapestries provide a feast of flora and fauna for the eyes.
