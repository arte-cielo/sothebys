Overview

On 22 October 2014 Sotheby’s will offer a selection of important English and European furniture and decorations including a number of fine private collections such as the Estate of Andrew Gordon and a curated group of late Louis XVI furnishings from an important French collection. Highlights of the auction comprise a matched pair of very large German Rococo silvered mirrors, a fine George III Chinese black lacquer-mounted breakfront cabinet attributed to Thomas Chippendale and a Flemish tapestry depicting Perseus fighting Polydectes from the workshop of Jacob van der Goten.
